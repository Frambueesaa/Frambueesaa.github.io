Title: Confinio: Reality Prison

Developer: Enthariel Games

Development Time: November 2023 - December 2024

Game Engine: Unity 2022.3.54f1.

Synopsis: Dive into Confinio: Reality Prison, a 2D graphic novel where darkness envelops your past and uncertainty guides your steps. Awaken in a cell with no memory, where your decisions will shape your destiny.

You’ve just awakened, confused, You look around, not knowing where you are. It looks like a cell, are you trapped? You don’t remember anything that happened.

How did you get here? What is going on? Too many unanswered questions. Are there others here? Would you trust them? Would you let them help? So many decisions to make.

Genres and Tags: Visual Novel, 2D, Psychological Horror.
